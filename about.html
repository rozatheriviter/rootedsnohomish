Bridging the Gap

Connecting Snohomish County residents to essential services, dignity, and community support.

The Mission

Rooted: Snohomish was born out of a critical necessity. Finding essential resources in Snohomish County can be fragmented, creating a barrier for those seeking support north of King County.

While resources exist, they are often scattered across various platforms. Rooted: Snohomish aims to be a centralized, accessible digital hub specifically for Snohomish County. This tool bridges that gap, ensuring that residents can find the help they need without navigating a maze of disconnected information.

Who We Are

This guide is a volunteer-led initiative. We aim to bridge the gap for residents in Everett, Lynnwood, Marysville, and surrounding areas. Having witnessed firsthand the difficulty of navigating scattered information, this project aims to condense vital data into a single, searchable, and dignified platform.

We believe that access to information is a fundamental right. Whether you are looking for a hot meal, a safe place to sleep, or healthcare services, you deserve to find that information quickly, without barriers, and without judgment.
